ssc

Marking the culmination of my foundational education, I proudly hold the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) from St. Paul's High School, Dadar. This certificate signifies my successful completion of ten years of rigorous academic pursuits, laying the groundwork for future endeavors. My experience at St. Paul's High School not only equipped me with essential knowledge and skills, but also nurtured valuable values and personal growth. It serves as a testament to my dedication, perseverance, and the invaluable guidance received from my teachers and mentors.

hsc

Building upon the solid foundation of my SSC, I subsequently earned the Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) from M. D. College, Parel. This two-year period, encompassing grades 11 and 12, marked a period of deeper exploration and specialization in chosen subjects. The rigorous curriculum at M. D. College challenged me intellectually, honed my critical thinking skills, and prepared me for further academic challenges. I am grateful for the knowledgeable faculty and supportive environment that fostered my academic growth and personal development during this crucial juncture. Receiving the HSC signifies not only the successful completion of this stage of education but also my dedication to academic pursuits and pursuit of higher goals.

graduation

Following my HSC, I pursued a B.Com. degree at Bhavan's College, Girgaon. This rigorous program (13th-15th standards) instilled in me a deep understanding of commerce, preparing me for a flourishing career path. I'm proud to have successfully navigated this journey, acquiring the knowledge and skills to confidently enter the professional world.

My experience at St. Paul's High School not only equipped me with essential knowledge and skills, but also nurtured valuable values and personal growth. It serves as a testament to my dedication, perseverance, and the invaluable guidance received from my teachers and mentors.

This two-year period, encompassing grades 11 and 12, marked a period of deeper exploration and specialization in chosen subjects. The rigorous curriculum at M. D. I am grateful for the knowledgeable faculty and supportive environment that fostered my academic growth and personal development during this crucial juncture. Receiving the HSC signifies not only the successful completion of this stage of education but also my dedication to academic pursuits and pursuit of higher goals.

graduation

about me !

Ten years ago, the allure of commerce pulled me down a different path. But deep down, the tech in me tugged, whispering questions about website magic. Curiosity took hold, morphing into pixel-perfect obsession. HTML became my secret language, CSS my palette, and JavaScript my dance partner. As a graphic designer, my eye craved beauty, so I sketched a website dream, then hand-coded it into reality. The journey was rocky, frustration my frequent foe. Yet, with each hurdle cleared, my front-end skills gained momentum. Now, the siren song of back-end beckons, promising new depths to explore. This is my story, a tale of curiosity, code, and pixel-perfect passion, still being written, one line at a time.
